**Awakening to the River**

You arrived in this World as a baby,
you didn't yet know anything about the World,
you are weak, you are struggling, prone to falling sick again and again,
you cannot do anything but continue to exist,
learning more about the World,
slowly comprehending and
making sense of things that didn't make any sense earlier,
you learn to recognize your parents,
you learn to recognize that you are safe with them.
You slowly learn to walk,
you slowly learn to talk,
as your loving parents encourage you in every step.
You try to walk,
you fall,
you get hurt,
you cry,
but continue at it nonetheless.
you try to talk,
you struggle to make sounds,
make funny noises that don't make any sense,
even pronouncing simple words is too much for you.
Yet, you continue at it,
your sense of wonder and curiosity towards the World keeps on growing.

You learn to notice that no two things in this World are exactly the same,
yet there are also similarities to be found in anything and everything.
The sounds you hear?
No two sounds are alike
people you see?
no two persons alike,
no two moments in time,
no two experiences in life,
yet a continuity of experience connects them all....connects us all human beings tied together with same Fate, same Destiny, same weight that is both a burden borne by us and something that adds meaning to our lives and push us towards progress.
We all are born, we strive for our goals,
and eventual death is also the only permanent reality that we cannot escape from.
One beginning, One goal, One End.

Our experiences color our reality,
Common inspirations connect together our hearts. And the ultimate desire to leave a legacy by producing something meaningful that outlasts us, is also all the same.
"The lamps are different, but the Light is the same."
And so, the River flows through us all,
we just need to awaken the "Heart".
